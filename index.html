Before you begin
  • Answer every question as well as you can. Questions start easier and get more challenging toward the end.
  • Some questions say “Select all that apply” — there may be more than one correct choice.
  • For short-answer questions, type just the number (a fraction like -5/6 is fine where asked).
  • The timer is a guide only — it will not submit for you. Press Submit when finished to see your score, a skill-by-skill breakdown, and worked solutions for anything missed.
